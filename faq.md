# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I play archives without downloading them?

### Apple

1. Install [Mega](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mega/id706857885) on your phone. *(You may or may not have to create an account)*
2. Go to the [HBNI archive](https://hbniaudioarchive.herokuapp.com) and press any archive you want to listen to.
3. You will be redirected ot the [Mega](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mega/id706857885) app.
4. You can directly play the broadcast that way without downloading it to your device.

### Android

1. Install [Mega](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mega.privacy.android.app&hl=en_US&gl=US) on your phone. *(You may or may not have to create an account)*
2. Go to the [HBNI archive](https://hbniaudioarchive.herokuapp.com) and press any archive you want to listen to.
3. You will be redirected ot the [Mega](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=mega.privacy.android.app&hl=en_US&gl=US) app.
4. You can directly play the broadcast that way without downloading it to your device.

### Windows (Computer)

- Just press on any archive on the [HBNI archive](https://hbniaudioarchive.herokuapp.com), you will be redirected to [mega.nz](https://mega.nz), from there you can just press play.

## Where are the archives stored?

- The archives (as of 2022-07-12) are stored on a file hosting website called [mega.nz](https://mega.nz).
- The folder where all archives are stored online is [here](https://mega.nz/folder/CxF1AQwS#rucPTrUDuhHE5I76NtpkLA).
- All data on each broadcast (date, length, host, description, etc...) can be found [here](https://github.com/TheCodingJsoftware/HBNI-Audio-Stream-Recorder/blob/master/downloadLinks.json).
    - This data is what the archive refers from, whatever changes in this file, changes on the [HBNI archive](https://hbniaudioarchive.herokuapp.com).
    - However, some of this data such as the [graphs](https://github.com/TheCodingJsoftware/HBNI-Audio-Stream-Recorder/blob/master/graph.png) or listeners count is not publicly available, although if you wish to access it please email me at: jared@pinelandfarms.ca
